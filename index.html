Driveway Drainage

A better driveway starts with proper drainage.

Water is one of the biggest causes of driveway damage. When it runs down the drive or collects in low areas, it can wash away stone, create potholes, and soften the base. We look at the driveway and surrounding terrain to identify practical ways to help water move away from the traveled surface.

  • Reshaping and crowning to help shed water
  • Correcting low areas where water collects
  • Restoring roadside ditches and swales
  • Cleaning around existing culverts
  • Stabilizing soft or frequently washed-out areas
Drainage improvements are based on visible site conditions and practical construction experience. Engineered drainage design, permitting, and work within regulated waterways are not included unless specifically arranged.
